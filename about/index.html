Hi, I'm Graham

I'm a Product Designer and Front-end Developer at PCL Construction

What I Do

I work for PCL Construction as a Product Designer and Front-End Developer. I’ve always had a fascination with technology and an appreciation for design. I studied Computer Systems Technology at SIAST (Saskatchewan Polytechnic) and even then, I valued design as much as development.

I’ve been on a journey to figure out how to scratch the itch of creativity. I love the satisfaction that comes with coding a fully-functioning app, but it’s the need for creative expression and human understanding that led me to design.

Who I Am

I live in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada but I’m originally from Regina, Saskatchewan. I love the city I live in but I will always bleed green and white (Go Riders!). I play the drums, I collect vinyls (is it wrong that I buy them for the coloured editions?), and I love taking road trips. I’m always willing to try new foods and I want the people I love to appreciate spicy food as much as I do.
